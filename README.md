# India Tech Job Market Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Indian technology job market using Python, SQL, MySQL, and Power BI.

The objective is to identify hiring trends, top recruiting cities, role demand, company ratings, and experience requirements across India's technology sector.

---

## Tech Stack

- Python
- Pandas
- MySQL
- SQL
- Power BI

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains 1,554 technology job postings across 159 Indian cities.

Key attributes include:

- Job Title
- Company
- Location
- Experience Required
- Company Rating

---

## ETL Pipeline

### Extract
Collected job market data.

### Transform
- Removed duplicates
- Handled missing values
- Standardized locations
- Cleaned job titles
- Processed ratings

### Load
Loaded cleaned data into MySQL for analysis.

---

## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Job Postings
- Total Tech Cities
- Maximum Experience Required
- Average Company Rating
- Job Role Market Share

---

## Key Insights

- Bengaluru leads India in technology hiring demand.
- Data Analyst roles represent a major share of openings.
- Hiring demand is concentrated in metropolitan cities.
- Experience requirements remain relatively low for many openings.
