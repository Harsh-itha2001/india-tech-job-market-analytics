## Project Overview

India's tech hiring landscape is highly fragmented across cities, roles, and experience levels. This project analyzes **1,554 job postings across 159 cities** using Python, SQL, MySQL, and Power BI to uncover hiring patterns, role demand distribution, and regional employment trends across India's technology sector.

---

## Business Problem

Job seekers and analysts lack a clear, data-backed view of where tech hiring is concentrated and which roles dominate demand. The objective was to identify:

- Which cities are the top tech hiring hubs
- Which job roles dominate the market
- What experience levels are most in demand
- How company ratings vary across postings

## Key Insights

### 🏙️ Top Cities for Tech Jobs
| City | Job Postings |
|---|---|
| Bengaluru | 261 |
| NCR Region | 245 |
| Mumbai | 196 |
| Hyderabad | 133 |
| Pune | 100 |
| Remote | 90 |

Bengaluru alone accounts for the highest concentration of tech job postings in India.

### 📊 Job Role Market Share
| Role | Postings | Market Share |
|---|---|---|
| Other Tech Roles | 676 | 43.5% |
| Data Analyst | 595 | 38.29% |
| Software / Data Engineer | 160 | 10.3% |
| Data Scientist | 123 | 7.92% |

**Data Analyst roles represent 38.29% of all tech postings** — making it one of the most in-demand roles in the market.

### 📋 Overall Market Summary
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Job Postings | 1,554 |
| Total Tech Cities | 159 |
| Max Experience Required | 1 Year |
| Avg Company Rating | 3.58 |

Most positions require **0–1 years of experience** — highly accessible for freshers and early-career analysts.
